Biography

I am a Professor in the School of Computer and the director of the Data mining and Information Retrieval Laboratory, Guangdong University of Technology. I received my B.S. degree in Applied Mathematics and Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from South China University of Technology in 2005 and 2010, respectively. My research interests cover various topics, including causality, deep learning, and their applications. I am a recipient of the National Science Fund for Excellent Young Scholars, Natural Science Award of Guangdong, and so on awards. I am served as the area chair of ICML 2022, senior PC for AAAI 2019-2022, IJCAI 2019-2022, and so on. I am now a member of ACM and CCF, and a senior member of IEEE.
